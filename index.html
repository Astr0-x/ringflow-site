Protocol Intel (FAQ)

How fast can we be live?

Once you sign up and provide your business details, our team begins the training protocol. Most agents are live and answering calls in under 24 hours.

Is there a contract?

No contracts. RingFlowHQ operates on a month-to-month subscription. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time.

Can callers tell it's an AI?

We use low-latency, high-fidelity neural voices. While we don't hide the AI nature if asked, most callers find the interaction so helpful and natural that they don't even question it.

What happens if the AI gets stuck?

You can set "guardrails." If the AI encounters a question it doesn't know, it can be programmed to seamlessly transfer the call to your personal cell or take a detailed message for you to return later.
